# THE-MATHS-GAME
Maths Rocket: Space Explorer
A fun interactive mental math game built with Vanilla JavaScript, CSS and HTML. Solve equations to launch your rocket through the stratosphere!

Features -
Dynamic Difficulty: Level 1 focuses on simple addition/subtraction while higher levels introduce multiplication and larger numbers.

Speed Bonus: The faster you answer the more points you earn.

Streak Multiplier: Chain correct answers together to trigger "Fire Streaks" and massive point boosts.

Visual Progress: A real-time progress bar shows how close you are to completing your 10-question mission.

Fun Facts: Learn interesting mathematical trivia (like the origin of zero) on the results screen.

Mobile Friendly: Fully responsive design that works on tablets and smartphones.

How to Play - 
Look at the Question: A math problem appears in the space bubble.

Beat the Clock: You have 15 seconds to pick the right answer from the grid.

Collect Stars: Your high scores are saved locally as "Stars" to track your improvement over time.

Reach Legend Status: Scoring over 1,200 points earns you the "LEGENDARY" rank.

 Technical Stack -
Frontend: HTML, CSS (Flexbox/Grid/Keyframes)

Logic: JavaScript 

Typography: 'Fredoka One' for a playful UI and 'Nunito' for readability.

Assets: Pure CSS/SVG animations .
